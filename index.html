After (V2): Positioned as a professional creative environment. "Create -> Refine -> Perfect." Dark mode, sleek typography, and trust signals.

03. Business Strategy

Growth & Market Expansion

B2B White Labeling

Developed a branded "Mastering Engine" for record labels and distribution platforms. This competes directly with MasterChannel, allowing partners to offer AI services under their own URL.

Community-Led Development

Launched a private Discord beta network. We use this loop to rapid-test features like the "Advanced Settings" before pushing to the main branch.

Contextual Education Strategy

Instead of a static "Help Center," we built education into the workflow. Interactive modals appear when users hover over advanced controls (like Saturation or EQ).

The Impact: Reduced support tickets by 40% and increased feature adoption.

Market Penetration: Unlocking India

High traffic, low conversion. My analysis found the RBI E-Mandate was blocking card payments.

The Fix: Pivoted to a UPI-native payment stack via Paddle.

Result: Captured 80% of the market segment.

Revamped Unit Economics

Shifted from a strict subscription model to a Credit-Based System. This aligned price with value for bedroom producers who don't mix every day.
